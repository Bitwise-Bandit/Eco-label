# About
Eco-Label is an open-source platform that helps users understand whatâ€™s really in their food. It removes all filters and tells the objective truth about the popular food products and their ingredients. By combining barcode scanning and a crowdsourced additives database, Eco-Label makes chemical safety data accessible and transparent. Its food safety metrics aren't baseless claims of a corp but the ones backed by research and approved by WHO.

# Features
- Shows all ingredients used in food products
- Relies on both crowdsourced data and barcode scanning to give accurate info
- Warns about harmful chemicals or preservatives used.
- Shows the safe limit for each chemical used.
- Warns about any allergens contained.
